The idea for Black History Month started in the USA with the work of Carter G Woodson.
He was a brilliant historian and in 1912 he became the second African American.
To where he got a doctorate from Harvard University.
He was the son of people who had been enslaved.
Where he saw how the role of his people in American history was being ignored.
He started 'Negro History Week' because he believed that teaching black history was essential to give descendants of African people a sense of pride and identity and to protect them within wider society.
